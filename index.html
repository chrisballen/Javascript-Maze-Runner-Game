Game Rules:

The object of the game is to move your brown smiley face through the maze and exit the maze through the red door. If a blue monster touches you, the life you know is no more and you are dead. You can only run within the maze. You will have 5 mins to get through the maze. If you do not make it through the maze in 5 mins the life you know will be no more. Try and eat cake to score more points! The more monsters you add the higher the score multiplier

Good luck and stay alive!
